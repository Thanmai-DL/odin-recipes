How to prepare Raita

Description

Raitas have always been an indispensable part of Indian food. Growing up, there was always a raita on our lunch for most days.

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Before, you start peel the cucumber and grate it using a grater.
  2. Then add the grated cucumber to the yogurt and mix.
  3. Stir in the chopped mint leaves and mix.
  4. Garnish with mint and serve cucumber raita as a side with any Indian meal or with biryanis and pulao.
